Our Core Interventions
FREE Daily Tutorials
Providing free daily tutorials in Mathematics and English for students in underserved African communities
FREE Digital Skills Training
Empowering underprivileged African students with lucrative digital skills to set them up for success in a tech-driven world.
FREE Public Library
We provide students in underserved African communities with free access to a well stocked library—a vital resource for learning and research.
FREE Mentorship Program
We match students with accomplished professionals for one on one mentorship to help them navigate life and make informed decisions.
