Our Core Interventions
FREE Daily Tutorials
Providing free daily tutorials in Mathematics and English for students in underserved African communities
FREE Digital Skills Training
Empowering underprivileged African students with lucrative digital skills to set them up for success in a tech-driven world.
FREE Public Library
We provide students in underserved African communities with free access to a well stocked library—a vital resource for learning and research.
FREE Mentorship Program
We match students with accomplished professionals for one on one mentorship to help them navigate life and make informed decisions.
Career Enrichment Programs
We organize FREE seminars and webinars for students, where they meet with professionals from different works of life for career guidance.
What have
we done with
your help?
We’ve invested over $200,000 to bring more than 13 transformative projects to life, impacting over 10,000 underprivileged people across seven under-resourced African communities. In Lagos's largest community, home to over 2 million residents, we established a state-of-the-art educational resource center that provides access to cutting-edge learning resources. In Jeida, a rural village near Abuja with a population of 1,600, we collaborated with other organizations to establish the first school, creating new opportunities for children to thrive and pursue their dreams. .
